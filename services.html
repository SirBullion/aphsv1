Our Services

At All People Holistic Support, we deliver culturally informed, person-centred services that support independence, wellbeing, and connection to community across the Northern Territory. Our approach is grounded in respect, cultural safety, and practical support tailored to each person's needs.

NDIS Support Services

  • Personal care and daily living assistance
  • High intensity support within approved scope
  • Community participation and social support
  • Life skills development and capacity building
  • Household tasks and domestic assistance
  • Transport and support to attend appointments and activities
  • Supported Independent Living (SIL) and shared living support
  • Accommodation and tenancy-related support
  • Group and centre-based activities

Youth Engagement

  • Mentoring and positive role modelling
  • Life skills support for young people
  • Cultural connection and safe engagement
  • Support to build confidence, resilience, and independence

Liaison & Community Outreach

  • Community liaison and culturally informed support
  • Advocacy and assistance navigating services
  • Support in regional and remote communities
  • Connection to culture, community, and trusted networks

Who We Support

We support individuals, families, and communities across Darwin and the wider Northern Territory. Our services are inclusive, culturally respectful, and designed to respond to the real needs of the people we work with.

NDIS Participants

We provide personalised support to NDIS participants to help build capacity, strengthen independence, and achieve goals through services that are flexible, respectful, and participant-focused.

Young People

Through mentoring and youth engagement, we support young people to build life skills, confidence, positive routines, and stronger pathways for the future.

Families

We work alongside families by providing practical, culturally appropriate support that strengthens wellbeing, respects identity, and promotes positive outcomes for children, young people, and caregivers.

Communities

Our liaison and outreach services help bridge the gap between services and communities by supporting communication, cultural understanding, and stronger connections with local supports, Elders, and organisations.
