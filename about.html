About Us

All People Holistic Support is an Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander-owned, community-led organisation delivering culturally informed NDIS support, youth mentoring, and community liaison services across the Northern Territory.

Based in Darwin, we work alongside individuals, families, and communities to provide support that is respectful, practical, and grounded in culture. Our services are designed to empower people to live independently, strengthen connections to community and Country, and access the support they need to thrive.

We proudly support communities across Darwin, Arnhem Land, and remote areas of the Northern Territory. Our approach centres Aboriginal leadership, cultural understanding, and genuine relationships to ensure services reflect the real needs of the people we serve.

Why Choose Us

  • Culturally Grounded & Informed: Our services are shaped by Aboriginal knowledge, lived experience, and a strong connection to culture and Country.
  • Holistic Support: We support the whole person, focusing on wellbeing, independence, community participation, and meaningful outcomes.
  • Aboriginal Owned & Community Led: As an Aboriginal-owned provider, we understand the needs of our communities and work to deliver services built on trust, respect, and cultural safety.

Our Vision

To empower Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people through culturally safe and community-led services that strengthen independence, wellbeing, and connection to culture.

Our Values

  • Cultural Safety: Respecting identity, language, culture, and traditional ways of knowing.
  • Empowerment: Supporting individuals to build independence and achieve their goals.
  • Respect: Honouring every person's journey, experiences, and community connections.
  • Connection: Grounding our work in relationships, kinship, and connection to Country.

Who We Support

We support NDIS participants, young people needing mentorship, and families seeking culturally appropriate support and outreach services across Darwin and remote Northern Territory communities.
