About All People Holistic Support

We are an Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander-owned provider delivering culturally safe, person-centred support for individuals, families, and communities across Darwin and surrounding areas.

Our work brings together disability support, cultural understanding, community liaison, and practical service delivery to help participants build independence and stay connected to what matters most.

Our Services

We provide flexible support tailored to participant goals, needs, and community context.

Personal Care Support

Assistance with daily personal activities, routine care, and participant wellbeing.

Community Participation

Support to access community, appointments, activities, and social connection.

Life Skills Development

Building confidence and independence through everyday living and practical skill development.

Household Support

Help with household tasks that support safe, clean, and stable living environments.

High Intensity Support

Support for participants with more complex care needs, delivered within approved NDIS scope.

Community Liaison & Youth Mentoring

Support that strengthens engagement, advocacy, cultural connection, and trusted relationships.
